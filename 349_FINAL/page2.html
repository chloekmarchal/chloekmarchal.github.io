How does AI design translate cultural values into tangible experience?

AI operates based on datasets, and these datasets are hosted in the cloud. The cloud is a graphic representation of data storage that implies lightness, ephemerality, and transparency. Under the guise of this transparency, the material consequences of AI disappear. For the continuation of late liberal capitalism, the hiddenness of industry and work is crucial.

Questioning the status quo of technology design.

Artists, designers, and social scientists have been using design as a tool to subvert techno-fetishist narratives. From Japanese Chindogu device art, to Indigenous technologists' AI ethics manifestos, alternatives to the post-modern aesthetic can be found outside the mainstream narrative of AI.
